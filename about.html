About MatchDekho

Welcome to MatchDekho — your ultimate free destination for live cricket streaming. We are passionate cricket fans who built this platform to make cricket accessible to everyone, everywhere, for free.

Who We Are

MatchDekho is an independent cricket streaming aggregator platform. We bring together publicly available cricket streams, live scores, match schedules, and highlights into one easy-to-use platform so that cricket fans never miss a single ball.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple — to provide free, fast, and reliable access to live cricket for fans across India and around the world. Whether it's IPL, international matches, T20 leagues, or domestic tournaments, we've got you covered.

What We Offer

✅ Live cricket streaming links from publicly available sources

✅ Today's match schedule with start times

✅ Upcoming match previews

✅ Cricket highlights and replays

✅ Mobile-friendly, fast, and completely free

Our Team

MatchDekho is run by a small team of cricket enthusiasts under the Distribution Team MatchDekho banner. We are not affiliated with any official cricket board or broadcasting company.
